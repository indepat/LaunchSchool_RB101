How can we add the family pet "Dino" to our usual array:

```ruby
flintstones = %w(Fred Barney Wilma Betty BamBam Pebbles)
```

<ins>**solution**</ins>:

```ruby
flintstones = %w(Fred Barney Wilma Betty BamBam Pebbles)
flintstones << "Dino"
#=> ["Fred", "Barney", "Wilma", "Betty", "BamBam", "Pebbles", "Dino"]
```
